Overview

    Ultimate Triple Connection with Multiple Compatibility

    The Daxa Saturn ST1 headset combines three connectivity (cable, 2.4GHz dongle, and bluetooth) to make it easy for users to connect this headset with a variety of devices without delay.

    Convenience for Mobile Gamers

    The Daxa Saturn ST1 headset embeds a type-C connector on a 2.4GHz dongle that allows users to directly connect this headset to an Android mobile phone without a converter. Of course, this feature will make users more comfortable to play and maximize use anywhere

    Detachable Dual-Microphone

    The Daxa Saturn ST1 headset is supported by dual microphones, which are built-in and microphone sticks which certainly add to the user's style, so that when not needed can be stored. If the user is outside the house without carrying a microphone stick, there is no need to worry because the microphone can still be used because there is a built-in microphone with good quality.

    Comfortable Over-Ear Type Earcups

    Weighing just 165 grams, G435 is light and comfortable enough to wear all day.

    Large Driver Diameter

    The Daxa Saturn ST1 headset is supported by a pair of 50mm diameter drivers in producing sound. The large diameter of the driver serves to maximize the sound output produced.
